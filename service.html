Our Services

Employee Management

Our Employee Management module allows HR administrators to manage employee personal and professional details with ease. Administrators can create, update, or delete employee records, while employees can view their own details and track their personal information in a secure environment.

Payroll Services

Our Payroll Services ensure accurate and timely payroll processing, including salary distribution, tax calculation, and payslip generation. Employees can view and download their payslips, while administrators can manage salary records and ensure compliance with company financial policies.

Performance Monitoring

Track and assess employee performance with our performance monitoring tools. Administrators can track individual performance metrics and project completion, helping organizations monitor employee contributions towards company goals.

Project Tracking

Our project tracking feature allows employees and administrators to manage current and past project information, including client names, project timelines, and reporting managers. Ensure that all projects are completed on time and within budget, with full transparency of employee assignments.

Attendance Management

Keep track of employee attendance records, including leaves, working hours, and overtime. Our system provides accurate attendance monitoring to ensure payroll consistency and overall efficiency.

Recruitment Assistance

Streamline your recruitment process with our integrated recruitment tools. Manage job postings, candidate applications, and track progress from application to onboarding with ease.

Employee Self-Service

Enable employees to manage their own profiles, view payslips, request time off, and update personal information through the self-service portal, reducing administrative overhead.
